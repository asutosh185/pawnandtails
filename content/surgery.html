Surgery

Our Operation theatre is state of an art facility which is fully equipped with specially designed hygienic operating table, imported gaseous anesthetic machine (along with integrated ventilator to monitor gas delivery, respiratory CO2 and maintenance of O2 levels). Standard operating protocols are followed with regard to sterility, monitoring and procedures.

Our experienced veterinary surgeon at Paws N Tails can perform minor soft tissue surgeries like spaying, castration and ear procedures to complex major surgical procedures like Gastric volvulus dilation, intestinal anastomosis, urinary tract emergencies, hernia repairs and casearian section. A technician remains with the pet throughout its procedure and until it is fully awake and recovered.
