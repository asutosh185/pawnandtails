DENTAL CARE

At Paws N Tails we have experienced veterinarian with specialized equipment (Ultrasonic Dental Scalar) to identify, treat and guide prevention of dental problems with appropriate diagnosis and remedies. The equipment helps removing the rotten or damaged teeth and dental plaques with specialized drills and burrs. Our veterinarian regularly attends continuing professional development training and seminars to be updated with latest dental procedures.
