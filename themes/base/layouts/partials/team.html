The vet and his awesome team

Dr Neralla Sridhar

M.V.Sc(Surgery), MISVS, MIAEP
Chief Veterinary Surgeon

Dr Sridhar has 15 years of experience as Veterinary Surgeon and has worked with different organizations all over the world.

His career started as a teaching associate for postgraduate students in the city of Bangalore. He then relocated to Bahrain, where he was providing expert consultation for the large stud farms with horses and other livestock. After a four year stint, he eventually went on to work at Saudi Arabia where he was actively involved in the treatment and surgeries for the small animals.

He returned to India to work for the Royal Turf Club in Kolkatta offering treatments and surgeries for the race horses stationed in the club.

Despite all this experience, his true calling came when he set up his own pet clinic in the city of destiny. He has been instrumental in bringing Pawsandtails the glory it has earned today.

He says “ My mission in life is not merely to survive, but to thrive; and to do so with some passion, some compassion, some humor, and some style. …”. Thats Doctor Sridhar who lives with his beautiful wife and lovely daughter in the coastal city of Visakhapatnam.

Mrs.N.Sirisha

M.Sc (Environmental studies)
Partner Paws N tails

An environmentalist by education, Sirisha is the managing partner of the clinic as well as the lovely wife of the vet.

After working with an MNC in Bangalore for a few years, she also took up a short teaching stint in a school. Sirisha eventually joined Pawsandtails and is its lifeline today. Over the years, she quickly picked up the nuances of the trade and today she wears multiple hats - manager, advisor, vet assistant, cashier and everything beyond that.

She's a doer and is the lady behind the well kept premises of the clinic. She goes to great lengths to ensure the clinic is clean and hygienic for the pets and their parents.
